How to Use AI MAIS

Getting Started

AI MAIS is your comprehensive medical intelligence platform. Choose from our three main analysis tools:

Available Tools

  • Advanced AI Analysis: Upload your medical data and get AI-powered insights with detailed reports and visualizations.
  • Smart Quality Assurance: Ensure your data meets medical standards with automated quality checks and validation.
  • Smart Marketing & CRM: Analyze market trends, customer data, and R&D intelligence for strategic planning.

Quick Tips

  • Start with the Advanced AI Analysis for general medical data processing
  • Use Quality Assurance before submitting critical reports
  • Explore Marketing & CRM for business intelligence needs
  • Check "Other Uses" for additional specialized applications

Voice Assistant

Use the voice assistant button to ask questions in English or Arabic. Simply tap the microphone icon and speak your question.

Support

For technical support or questions, contact our team through the platform or refer to the detailed documentation in each tool section.
